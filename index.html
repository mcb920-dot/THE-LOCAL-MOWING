Who We Are

Your Neighbor.
Your Lawn Guy.

The Local Mowing is locally owned and based in Centerton, AR. We're not a franchise, not a call center — when you call, you reach us directly.

Think of it like a great haircut. Anyone can cut hair, but the barber who takes their time, studies your head, and dials in the shape — that's the one you go back to. We approach every lawn the same way.

The first few visits are about learning your yard: the grain, the edges, the trouble spots. We're building something, not just running through it. Every visit includes mowing, edging & trimming, and blowing — no exceptions.
